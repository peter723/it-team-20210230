Design & display

As you hold iPhone 13 Pro Max in your hand, you immediately notice the spacious 6.7-inch Super Retina XDR display. The larger screen estate creates an immersive viewing experience, so you can enjoy your favourite Netflix series wherever you are. On top of this, the display is made with the durable Ceramic Shield front, which protects your smartphone from scratches

Dual lens camera

Turn the smartphone over and you’ll find an advanced trio camera system, including a 12MP wide lens, 12MP telephoto lens and 12MP ultrawide lens. Take detailed snaps at any time of day thanks to the brilliant Night Mode setting, in which iPhone 13 Pro Max will automatically adjust your photograph’s brightness in the dark. Not to mention the superb Portrait Lighting feature.

Li-ion battery

For long-lasting, reliable and efficient performance, iPhone 13 Pro Max is built with the non-removable Li-ion battery. With a battery capacity of 4352 mAh, you’ll have plenty of battery life to get you through the day without worrying about where the nearest plug-in adapter is. Plus, its fast-charging time means you won’t need to power up for very long either

A15 Bionic Chip

The engine behind the lightning quick speed is the powerful A15 Bionic chip, which lets you smoothly multi-task, even with the most demanding applications, without freezing. Mobile gamers will also appreciate iPhone 13 Pro Max’s ability to deal with graphics-intensive games with ease.
